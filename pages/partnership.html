Partnership

Join me in this calling through prayer and support

Why Partnership Matters

This calling is bigger than me — it's something God is doing in Manila, and I'm saying yes to being part of it. But I can't do it alone. I need a team of people who will pray with me, encourage me, and support me financially so I can show up in the community God has called me to.

When you partner with me, you're not just giving. You're joining the story. You're investing in real people, real relationships, and real change in real lives — through your prayers, your generosity, and your belief in what God is doing.

Financial Partnership

Financial partners make it possible for me to live, build relationships, and serve in Manila long-term. Your support helps provide housing, food, transportation, ministry involvement, and the everyday needs that allow me to show up — in homes, in classrooms, in the daily rhythms of life in Manila.

📅 Monthly Support

Monthly giving creates stability and allows me to serve consistently and sustainably. A gift of $25 covers a week of transportation to ministry sites. $50 covers a month of ministry materials. Every dollar goes directly to the work.

My Story

How God led me to Manila

Who I Am

I'm Ivy — a young woman from Washington learning to follow Jesus wherever He leads. I never expected my yes to take me across the world, but God has a way of writing stories we never would've written for ourselves.

My Calling

The Trip That Changed Everything

On my first missions trip to Manila with Kids International Ministries, I went expecting to serve. What I found was something I wasn't prepared for. I met kids and families whose joy ran deeper than their circumstances and whose faith was steady and real. I watched a community hold onto hope in ways that made my own faith feel small and new again. I went home different. I couldn't stop thinking about the families I'd met.

That trip planted something in me. A question I couldn't quiet: What if I went back — not for a week, but for years?

The Call Grew Stronger

When I returned the next year, God kept bringing me back to the same certainty: This is where I want you. I had never heard His voice that clearly. I felt Him inviting me into something bigger than I'd imagined for myself.

I said yes. And since then, I have been praying, planning, and preparing to return.

Why Manila?

Manila is one of the most densely populated cities in the world — layered, complex, and full of people whose lives are shaped by both resilience and real hardship. Many families face poverty, limited opportunities, and daily uncertainty.

Kids International Ministries serves right in the middle of these communities, offering education, discipleship, feeding programs, and consistent love. This is where I feel called to be — walking alongside kids and families, learning the culture, building relationships that take years, not weeks.

Kids International Ministries, Cuatro, Manila

Where I'm Serving: Kids International Ministries

Kids International Ministries (KIM) is a long-term, community-focused ministry serving families and children in Manila. Their mission is simple: to share the love of Jesus through consistent presence, practical support, and genuine relationships.

KIM partners with local communities through education, feeding programs, discipleship, and daily ministry life — meeting both spiritual and physical needs of the people in the community.

KIM Serves the Community Through:

📚 Education

Supporting students through school programs and academic mentorship.

🍽️ Feeding Programs

Providing meals to kids and families in need.

📖 Discipleship

Bible studies, youth groups, and spiritual mentorship.

🤝 Community Outreach

Building relationships with families and neighbors.

What My Role Looks Like

As a long-term missionary with KIM, I'm committed to showing up — week after week, in homes and classrooms and community gatherings, building relationships that take years, not weeks. I invest in daily ministry activities, support the organization's mission, and share the love of Jesus in presence, conversation, and daily life alongside the families I serve with KIM.

My Story

How God led me to Manila

Who I Am?

I'm Ivy — a girl from Washington learning to follow Jesus wherever He leads. I never expected my yes to take me across the world, but God has a way of writing stories we never would've written for ourselves.

My Calling

It all began on my first missions trip ever...

It all began on my first missions trip ever, to Kids International Ministries in Manila, Philippines. This trip changed everything. I went expecting to serve, but I was the one who was transformed. I met kids and families whose joy ran deeper than their circumstances. Whose faith was steady and real.

I saw a kind of community, resilience, and hope that I had never experienced before. Their joy, strength, and faith in the middle of hardship opened my eyes and softened my heart in ways I can't fully explain. My heart was instantly changed.

The Call Grew Stronger

Over time, that first spark grew into a calling. When I returned the next year, God placed a calling on my heart and kept confirming the same thing: "This is where I want you." I'd never heard His voice so clearly before. I felt Him inviting me into something bigger than I could have imagined for myself.

I didn't hesitate. I said yes. And since then, I have been praying, planning, and preparing to return.

Why Manila?

Manila is full of beauty, culture, and life — but also real challenges. Many families face poverty, limited opportunities, and daily uncertainty.

Kids International Ministries serves right in the middle of these communities, offering education, discipleship, feeding programs, and consistent love. This is where I feel called to be — walking alongside kids and families, building relationships, learning the culture, and sharing the love of Jesus with these people.

Manila, Philippines

Where I'm Serving: Kids International Ministries

Kids International Ministries (KIM) is a long‑term, community‑focused ministry serving families and children in Manila. Their heart is simple: to share the love of Jesus through consistent presence, practical support, and genuine relationships.

KIM partners with local communities through education, feeding programs, discipleship, and daily ministry life — meeting both spiritual and physical needs of the people in the community.

KIM Serves the Community Through:

📚 Education

Supporting students through school programs and academic mentorship.

🍽️ Feeding Programs

Providing meals to kids and families in need.

📖 Discipleship

Bible studies, youth groups, and spiritual mentorship.

🤝 Community Outreach

Building relationships with families and neighbors.

What My Role Looks Like

As a long-term missionary with KIM, I'm committed to being present in the community. I invest in relationships, participate in daily ministry activities, support the organization's mission, and share the love of Jesus through both words and actions. My work includes involvement in education programs, discipleship initiatives, community events, and the daily rhythms of life alongside the families we serve.
